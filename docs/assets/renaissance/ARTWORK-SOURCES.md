# Artwork sources

The three full-screen exhibition crops in this portfolio come from public-domain museum images.

- **A Woodland Road with Travelers** — Jan Brueghel the Elder, 1607. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, object 1975.1.124. Source: https://www.metmuseum.org/art/collection/search/435810. Public Domain image supplied through The Met Open Access program.
- **The Feast of the Gods** — Giovanni Bellini and Titian, 1514/1529. National Gallery of Art, Washington, object 1942.9.1. Source: https://www.nga.gov/artworks/1138-feast-gods. Open Access/Public Domain image served through the NGA IIIF API.
- **The Harvesters** — Pieter Bruegel the Elder, 1565. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, object 19.164. Source: https://www.metmuseum.org/art/collection/search/435809. Public Domain image supplied through The Met Open Access program.

Website crops are 3200 × 1800 WebP files, art-directed for widescreen display and sharpened lightly after Lanczos resampling. The original museum JPEG files are retained beside the crops for provenance and future recropping.
